DELIVERY & TRANSPORTING AT YOUR SERVICE

Moving Your Business Forward — One Delivery at a Time.

Domestic Delivery

At Karatroute Logistics, we understand the importance of timely and dependable delivery within national borders. Our Domestic Delivery service is built on speed, efficiency, and precision. Whether you're sending personal parcels or managing business logistics, we provide door-to-door delivery with real-time tracking and customer support. From urban centers to rural areas, we ensure your packages arrive safely and swiftly — because every mile counts when it comes to your satisfaction.

International Shipping

Expand your reach with confidence through our International Shipping solutions. Serving over 220 countries and territories, we take the hassle out of global logistics. Our team handles all aspects of international transport — including customs documentation, regulatory compliance, and last-mile delivery — so you can focus on your core business. With multiple freight options (air, sea, and express), reliable transit times, and proactive tracking, we’re your trusted partner in global commerce.

Security Vault

Protect what matters most with our Security Vault service — designed for clients who require top-tier storage for valuables, sensitive documents, or high-end inventory. Our secure facilities are equipped with 24/7 CCTV surveillance, biometric access control, and climate regulation to ensure the highest standards of safety. Whether you need short-term protection or long-term storage, Karatroute offers peace of mind with confidentiality, integrity, and care at the core of our service.

Warehousing Fulfillment

Scale your operations with ease using our Warehousing & Fulfillment solutions. We provide modern, strategically located warehouse facilities optimized for storage, inventory control, and fast order fulfillment. Perfect for e-commerce businesses, wholesalers, and distributors, our services include inventory management, packaging, labeling, and direct-to-customer shipping. With real-time visibility and seamless integration, we help you reduce overhead and deliver on customer expectations — every time.

Freight & Cargo Services

Whether you're moving a single container or managing large-scale shipments, our Freight & Cargo Services are tailored to meet your unique transportation needs. We offer a full range of freight solutions including air freight for speed, sea freight for cost efficiency, and land freight for regional logistics. Our experienced logistics coordinators manage everything — from route planning and freight booking to documentation and customs clearance. We ensure that your cargo is delivered safely, on time, and in full compliance with international standards.

Contact us to discuss how our services can support your business and streamline your logistics operations.
